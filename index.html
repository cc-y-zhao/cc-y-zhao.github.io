About Me

"Cecilia's work ethic, attention to detail, and analytical skills were exceptional. She brought an uncommon level of positivity to Client meetings and internal work group settings and was always willing to step up and take on tasks with little to no direction in a very high-paced environment."

^ These are the words of my former managing director, Chris Hughes, (see full recommendation here) from back in my investment banking days and they do a pretty good job of summing up who I am in a nutshell :)

A little over a year ago I left my job in investment banking in order to take some time to figure out what I wanted to do in life. I've always been a pretty pragmatic person, so in all honesty my initial motivations for venturing into software engineering were more thematically centered around acquiring in-demand skills and obtaining occupational stability. With that, I enrolled in App Academy and in 6 grueling and intensive months, learned full-stack web development. It was within that time that something unexpected happened - I fell in love with coding. As corny as it sounds, the joy and satisfaction that I feel when I solve a bug or see one of my app's completed features render on the screen are emotions I've never really experienced before. I'm incredibly happy to have found something that I truly enjoy doing and can't wait to hit the ground running! So if you're looking for someone hard working, efficient, proactive, and pleasant to be around - look no further!

Outside of work I enjoy playing with my cats (pictured on the right - yes, the orange one is as naughty as he looks), exploring San Francisco's restaurant scene, running, and hanging out with friends and family.
